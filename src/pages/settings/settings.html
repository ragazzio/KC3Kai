On-Screen Quest Translations
On-screen translations are "masking tapes" that hover on top of the game screen. It is safe and does not meddle with game files nor the network. Much like how pop-up ads are, its totally outside of the game's flash client.
Show on-screen english translations
Timer Allowance
Expedition and repairs are known to complete by at most, one minute before the intended time. This completion is affected by different factors and the actual time differs for every person. Usually it's 40 to 50 seconds so KC3改's default is 45 seconds. Set it to 60 seconds if you still get the full one minute free completion time.
seconds [ Set ]

Spoiler: Ship Construction Face
Some people do not want spoiler on their construction. If you're one of them, here's the option to turn off the faces in the construction timers in the Admiral's panel.
Show me ship faces in construction timers
Spoiler: Compass Result
Treading a normal map or event map involves spinning a compass. Contrary to popular belief, the result is actually already decided even before you spin it, thus the spin is just for animation. The admiral panel is able to show you these results for better decision making. This does not work for nodes that requires manual-click branching.
Show me compass results before spinning
Spoiler: Battle Prediction
Allow KC3改 to do its own calculation for battles to predict their ending HP. These must not be used as basis for continuing to next map since it's just a "preditction". When battle mechanics change, it is not guaranteed that KC3改's calculations are updated. You must wait for the map screen to show you actual HPs to be used as basis in proceeding. You do not want to be kuso.
Show me prediction HP bars
